King's Feast

Online sales start June 1st and end at 6pm the night of the feast.

These tickets include 3 main things:

1. General Admission to the faire which opens at 10:00 am.

2. Preferred seating for the 6:00 pm joust.

3. 7:00 pm feast with entertainment.

Seating will at long tables and we will try to put groups together. If you require group seating please come to the tent immediately after the joust.

The King's Feast is held in a large tent and it includes a multi-course meal with entertainment. Please choose the main course you want.

Tickets are valid for general admission both days of the faire.
